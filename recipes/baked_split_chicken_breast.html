Baked Split Chicken Breast

Description

This is a very easy but elegant recipe that looks like you've spent hours in the kitchen when you've barely lifted a finger! The base recipe yields 2 servings and the time spent baking the chicken is dependant on the thickness of the breast.

Feel free to scale up this recipe for more guests! The prep time is minimal regardless of whether you make 2 breasts or 20.

Ingredients

  • 2 large bone-in chicken breast halves with skin
  • ¼ cup extra-virgin olive oil
  • ½ teaspoon garlic, minced
  • ½ teaspoon coarse sea salt
  • ½ teaspoon cracked black pepper
  • ¼ teaspoon dried rosemary
  • ¼ teaspoon dried basil
